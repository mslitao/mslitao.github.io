Hierarchical Visualization of Fortune Global 500 @2019

The Fortune Global 500, also known as Global 500, is an annual ranking of the top 500 corporations worldwide as measured by revenue and the list is compiled and published annually by Fortune magazine.

The world’s 500 largest companies generated $32.7 trillion in revenues and $2.15 trillion in profits in 2019. Together, this year’s Fortune Global 500 companies employ 69.3 million people worldwide and are represented by 34 countries

Hierachical Visualization using D3

A Tree Diagram is a way of visually representing hierarchy in a tree-like structure. Typically the structure of a Tree Diagram consists of elements such as a root node, a member that has no superior/parent. Then there are the nodes, which are linked together with line connections called branches that represent the relationships and connections between the members. Finally, the leaf nodes (or end-nodes) are members who have no children or child nodes.
